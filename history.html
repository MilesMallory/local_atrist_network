History of the Local Art Scene

From murals to music, our city has long been a hub for creative expression. Here's a look at some key moments in our artistic history:

  • 1920s: Harlem Renaissance inspires local Black artists to find their voice.
  • 1960s: Civil Rights era art movements surge with powerful political messages.
  • 1980s: Street art and graffiti emerge as forms of protest and identity.
  • 2000s: Digital media changes the game for collaboration and exposure.
  • Today: Multicultural collectives and community-run galleries thrive.
